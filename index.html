Acerca de mí

Hey! I’m Aydin - thanks for taking the time to check out my portfolio!

I’m currently a second-year Computer Science student at the University of London and working as a Software Engineer at Zebra Technologies on an industrial placement.

I really love building meaningful projects that people genuinely enjoy using. From design system components to fully fledged platforms, I’ve worked on small and large-scale projects. My passion lies in writing code that helps people - whether that’s making everyday tasks easier or improving accessibility for components or projects. I’m always excited to learn new things and enjoy stepping outside of my comfort zone!

I’ve had the chance to work with a bunch of different technologies across all kinds of projects. I’ve built reusable components with TypeScript, Lit, and Flutter, helped maintain and grow a large business platform using React, and even got my hands dirty with unit testing. I'm also used to working within Agile teams, taking part in sprints and scrums to keep projects on track.

Outside of coding, I’ve also contributed to Zebra Technologies’ design blog, sharing a bit about what it’s like working in industry. You can check that out here if you're curious!: Zeta Design Blog
