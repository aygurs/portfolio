Hello, and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Aydin Gursoy, and I'm a 27-year-old junior front-end web developer with a strong interest in web and mobile development. Combining a solid educational foundation with a passion for self-learning, I strive to create high-quality projects with accessible, sleek designs.
